Optumera Promotion:
  A retail promotion is a persuasive marketing strategy designed to drive sales. Leveraging 
    the right retail promotions can help reach customers and increase revenue in business.
  Promotion planning and Management:
    Promotion is a short-term tactics that retailers generally use so that they can boost 
      the purchase of their product or service.
    Two different kinds of sales promotion:
      Customer promotion,
      Trade promotion
    You provide incentives to the end consumer so that you can make them buy the product or 
      service.
    Trade promotion which is so that manufacturer product can be kind of pushed through the 
      distribution channel mode.
  Reason for Promotion:
    Need to persuade consumers who are loyal to other brands,
    The products which are promoted has more sales and more reach with the customers,
    There is like increased financial performance for the retailers and marketers, it helps 
      you to beat the competition both with other retailers and overall, in the industry 
      level.
    It help you penetrate into newer markets, gain market share and reach your financial 
      targets.
  Objectives of promotion:
    to improve their revenues, 
    improve their profit margin, 
    sales margin, 
    improve the customer satisfaction, 
    increase the lifetime value of the customer
  Analyzing performance of a promotion gives insights into evolving customer preferences, 
    styles, and buying patterns.
  The basics of retail promotions revolve around four main factors:
    product, 
    price, 
    place,
    promotions.
